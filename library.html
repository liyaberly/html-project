Library & Learning Resources

The Central Library at Amal Jyothi College of Engineering is a hub of knowledge with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources. It serves as a vital learning space for students and faculty alike.

Library Facilities

  • Books & Journals: Over 50,000 books and 500+ national & international journals.
  • Digital Library: Access to IEEE, Springer, and other leading digital databases.
  • Research Support: Assistance for thesis writing, citations, and publishing.
  • Reading Areas: Silent study zones and group discussion rooms.
