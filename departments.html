Departments at Amal Jyothi College of Engineering

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple engineering disciplines. Each department is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and research facilities that foster academic excellence. Below is an overview of the various departments at our institution.

Undergraduate Departments

Department Description
Automobile Engineering Focuses on the design, manufacturing, and maintenance of automobiles, including emerging technologies such as electric vehicles.
Civil Engineering Provides knowledge in structural design, construction materials, geotechnical engineering, and urban planning.
Chemical Engineering Combines principles of chemistry and engineering to develop processes for industries like pharmaceuticals, food processing, and energy.
Computer Science and Engineering Focuses on software development, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and data science, with strong industry collaborations.
Electronics and Communication Engineering Explores signal processing, embedded systems, telecommunications, and wireless networking.
Electrical and Electronics Engineering Deals with power systems, electrical machines, renewable energy, and automation technologies.
Information Technology Focuses on web development, networking, cloud computing, and IT security.
Mechanical Engineering Covers manufacturing, robotics, thermodynamics, and material science.
Metallurgy Studies the properties of metals and their applications in various industries like aerospace, construction, and transportation.
